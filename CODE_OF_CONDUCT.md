# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Share knowledge openly.
- Support contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Spam
- Malicious content

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate contributions.
